## Typography

- Display: 'museo-slab', 'Rockwell', 'Iowan Old Style', Georgia, serif
- Body: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', system-ui, sans-serif
- Mono: 'JetBrains Mono', ui-monospace, 'SF Mono', Menlo, monospace

## Posture rules

1. Data-dense utility feel — information per square inch, not vibes
2. Tabular numerics everywhere; mono for IDs, codes, dates
3. Hairline borders, no shadows except modals/dropdowns
4. Teal accent at most 2x per screen: active nav item + primary CTA
5. Navy background for sidebar navigation — establishes brand presence
